# Impact Analysis

## Business Impact

- Finance could not rely on month-end reports without manual reconciliation
- Asset custody records temporarily lacked confidence
- Payroll and project costing risked delay if left unaddressed

---

## Operational Impact

- Additional manual validation was required
- ERP write operations for affected modules were temporarily restricted
- Engineering and operations time diverted to investigation

---

## What Was NOT Impacted

- No data was deleted or corrupted beyond consistency issues
- No unauthorized access or data exposure occurred
- Core ERP availability was unaffected

---

## Risk Assessment

| Risk Category | Assessment |
|--------------|------------|
| Financial Accuracy | Low |
| Operational Disruption | Low |
| Audit Readiness | Moderate |
| Reputational Exposure | Low |

---

## Summary

The impact was **correctness-related**, not availability-related.
Accuracy and trust — not uptime — were the primary concerns.

## Conclusion

While impact was limited, the incident revealed
a control gap that could have led to material risk
if not addressed.
