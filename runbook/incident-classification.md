# Incident Classification

Incidents are classified by business risk,
not technical complexity.

---

## SEV-1 (Critical)

Criteria:
- Financial reporting at risk
- Donor or regulatory exposure
- Widespread integrity violations

Response:
- Immediate escalation
- Executive notification

---

## SEV-2 (High)

Criteria:
- Integrity violation detected
- No external impact yet
- Automation alerts firing

Response:
- On-call response within SLA
- Containment required

---

## SEV-3 (Medium)

Criteria:
- Localized inconsistency
- No reporting impact

Response:
- Business-hours handling acceptable

---

## Rule

> When in doubt, classify higher.
