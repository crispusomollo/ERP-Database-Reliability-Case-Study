# Escalation Matrix

Defines who must be notified based on severity.

---

| Severity | Notify Immediately | Notify If Impact Confirmed |
|--------|-------------------|----------------------------|
| SEV-1 | CEO, Audit Chair, Legal | Board |
| SEV-2 | ERP Lead, Ops Manager | Finance |
| SEV-3 | ERP Team | None |

---

## Principle

> Escalate early. Silence is risk.
