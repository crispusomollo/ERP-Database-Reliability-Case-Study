# Sample Audit Walkthrough

## Audit Focus
Asset custody integrity and approval traceability

---

## Step 1: Risk Identification
Auditor reviews failure-modes/
to understand potential integrity risks.

---

## Step 2: Control Design
Auditor inspects prevention/
to confirm database-level enforcement.

---

## Step 3: Control Operation
Auditor reviews automation/
to confirm scheduled checks and alerting.

---

## Step 4: Incident Handling
Auditor inspects runbook/
and prior incident documentation.

---

## Step 5: Effectiveness
Auditor reviews metrics/
to confirm reduced risk and zero recurrence.

---

## Expected Outcome

Auditor can independently conclude
controls are designed, implemented, and effective.
