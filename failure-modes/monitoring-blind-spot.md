# Failure Mode: Monitoring Blind Spot

## Scenario
Integrity violations occur while monitoring
or alerting is unavailable or misconfigured.

## Risk
- Delayed detection
- Extended exposure window

## Detection
- Manual reconciliation
- Audit review

## Prevention
- Redundant monitoring paths
- Ownership and escalation mapping

## Recovery
- Restore monitoring
- Retroactive integrity validation

## Residual Risk
Medium

## Owner
Operations Lead

## Review Cadence
Annual
